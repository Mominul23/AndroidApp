Privacy Policy - Knowledge Hub

Effective Date: August 16, 2025

Welcome to Knowledge Hub! Your privacy is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, store, use, and protect your data when you use our educational quiz application.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Knowledge Hub respects user privacy. We do not collect sensitive personal information such as:

  • Your name, phone number, or address
  • Login credentials, account details, or passwords
  • Precise location or biometric identifiers

2. Local Data Storage

We only store necessary quiz-related data on your device using Shared Preferences:

  • School name (optional input)
  • Quiz progress, scores, and earned points
  • History of completed quizzes

Note: If you uninstall the app, all stored data (points, history, progress) will be permanently deleted.

3. How Knowledge Hub Works

Our application is designed for education and entertainment. Users can:

  • Participate in quizzes across multiple subjects
  • Answer within a time limit
  • Earn points and track their achievements

Important: The app is not gambling. Points have no cash value at this stage. They are for learning motivation and self-improvement only.

4. Points, Rewards & Future Updates

Currently, points are stored locally and only visible to the user. In future, we may introduce:

  • Reward systems (badges, ranks, or gift coupons)
  • Optional online account to sync progress
  • Leaderboard for friendly competition

Important: Any real rewards will follow Google Play’s Gambling & Rewards Policy to ensure compliance.

5. Permissions

Knowledge Hub requires minimal permissions. Core features work without:

  • Camera or microphone
  • Contacts or SMS
  • File storage access

Only internet access is needed for ads and app updates.

6. Advertising (Google AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display ads inside the app. AdMob may collect data such as:

  • Device type, model, and OS version
  • Approximate location (city/region)
  • Interactions with ads

Users can choose between personalized or non-personalized ads. Declining personalization will still allow ads but without tracking preferences.

Read more: Google Advertising Policy.

7. Data Security

We take data security seriously:

  • All quiz data stays on your device
  • We do not store personal data on external servers
  • Ad data is managed by Google under strict security policies

8. Children’s Privacy

Knowledge Hub is designed for all ages. However, since ads are displayed:

  • Children under 13 should use under parental guidance
  • We comply with Google’s Child-Directed Policy (COPPA)

9. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Uninstall the app at any time to delete local data
  • Choose whether or not to allow personalized ads
  • Request clarification about this policy

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy due to new features, AdMob updates, or legal requirements. Please check this page regularly.
