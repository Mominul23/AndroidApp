Privacy Policy - Knowledge Hub

Effective Date: August 16, 2025

Welcome to Knowledge Hub. Protecting your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information while you use our educational quiz application.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Knowledge Hub respects your privacy. We do not collect personal information such as:

  • Name, email, phone number, or address
  • Location or device identifiers (other than required by Google AdMob)
  • Login credentials or account information

2. Local Data Storage

Knowledge Hub uses local storage (Shared Preferences) to save:

  • Your school name (optional)
  • Quiz scores and earned points
  • Quiz progress and history

Important: All data is stored locally on your device. If you uninstall the app, any points, scores, or progress will be lost permanently.

3. How Knowledge Hub Works

The app allows users to:

  • Participate in subject-based quizzes
  • Answer questions within a set time limit
  • Earn points for correct answers

Note: Knowledge Hub is primarily an educational tool. It aims to enhance knowledge and learning skills while making the experience interactive and fun.

4. Points and Rewards

Points are earned based on quiz performance and stored locally. Currently, points are only visible on your device. Future updates may allow converting points into rewards, which will be implemented transparently and with user consent.

5. Permissions

The app does not require any sensitive permissions. Core features work without:

  • Camera or microphone
  • Contacts or storage access
  • Precise location (unless required by ads personalization, see below)

6. Advertising (Google AdMob)

Knowledge Hub uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect certain information automatically for the purpose of showing personalized or non-personalized ads, such as:

  • Device information (model, OS, identifiers)
  • General location (city or region)
  • Interaction with ads

Users in regions where required will be asked for consent to personalized ads. If you decline, you will still see non-personalized ads.

For more details, please review Google’s Advertising Policy.

7. Third-Party Services

Apart from AdMob, Knowledge Hub currently does not use third-party analytics or tracking services. Any future integrations will be updated in this Privacy Policy.

8. Data Security

Local data is stored securely on your device. Since we do not transfer user data to our servers, the risk of data breach is minimal. Ad data is managed by Google under their own security policies.

9. Children's Privacy

Knowledge Hub is suitable for all age groups. However, since we show ads, children under 13 (or the legal age in your region) should use the app under parental supervision. AdMob ensures compliance with Google’s Child-Directed Policy (COPPA).

10. Uninstalling the App

All local data (points, scores, history) is deleted when the app is uninstalled. Ads settings are managed by Google.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect app changes, AdMob policy updates, or legal requirements. Please check regularly for updates.
